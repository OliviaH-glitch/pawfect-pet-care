FAQ

Each pet is allowed to bring one toy, blanket, or pillow. Please remember these items will be around other animals, so do not bring anything you are overly-attached to. If your pet will require any medication during their stay, or the two hour period around their stay, be sure to drop off the medication with a label stating your name, the intended pet, and the time.

We invite all pet parents to check out our facility before making a decision! You can see for yourself just how cozy and well cared for your dog will be!

We require all residents to be up-to-date on all vaccinations. You must provide documention(either digital or physical) before we can board your pet. If your pet will require any medication, be sure to bring it with you during drop off!(See first question for more details)
